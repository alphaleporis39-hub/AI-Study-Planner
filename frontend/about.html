About StudyHub AI

We're on a mission to help every student study smarter, stay organised, and achieve their goals.

Our Mission

StudyHub AI was built with one goal: to give every student — from school to competitive exams — a powerful, free, AI-assisted tool that removes the chaos from studying and replaces it with clarity, structure, and momentum.

We believe great results come from consistent, well-planned effort. StudyHub AI makes that planning effortless.

The Story

StudyHub AI was created by Mohit Chaudhary, a developer passionate about education technology. After seeing how many students struggle with time management, last-minute cramming, and lack of structure, he built StudyHub AI to solve these problems for free.

The app launched on studyhubai.in and has been continuously improved based on student feedback, adding features like the Pomodoro timer, study analytics, exam countdown, and AI study tips.
