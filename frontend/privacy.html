Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2024  |  Effective: June 28, 2024

🔒 Short version: Your study data stays on your device (localStorage). We use Google Analytics for anonymous usage stats. We may use Google AdSense to display ads. We don't sell your data. Ever.

1. Introduction

Welcome to StudyHub AI ("we", "us", "our"), operated by Mohit Chaudhary and accessible at studyhubai.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our web application.

By using StudyHub AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account, you provide a username and password. No email address, real name, or payment information is required. This data is stored entirely in your browser's localStorage — it is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Study Data

All study-related data — subjects, timetables, goals, notes, exam dates, progress logs, and settings — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data does not leave your device.

2.3 Usage Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referral source (how you found us)

This data is collected anonymously and is used solely to improve StudyHub AI. Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

2.4 Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads based on your interests. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. You can manage ad personalisation at Google Ad Settings.

3. Cookies

StudyHub AI itself does not set cookies for its core functionality (we use localStorage instead). However, third-party services we integrate may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For usage tracking (_ga, _gid cookies)
  • Google AdSense: For ad personalisation

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not break the core StudyHub AI features, but may affect analytics and ad functionality.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and improve the StudyHub AI service
  • Understand usage patterns to prioritise new features
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Monitor and improve site performance

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your study data is stored in your browser's localStorage, which means:

  • Data is device-specific — it does not sync across devices
  • Clearing browser data will delete your StudyHub AI data
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers

We recommend keeping a personal backup of important schedule information.

6. Children's Privacy

StudyHub AI is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal information (email, name, etc.) and all data stays on the user's device, our service is safe for use by students of all ages.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data (it's in your browser's localStorage)
  • Delete: Clear your browser's localStorage to delete all data
  • Opt out of analytics: Use Google's opt-out browser add-on
  • Opt out of personalised ads: Use Google Ad Settings

8. Third-Party Links

StudyHub AI may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of StudyHub AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
